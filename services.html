Our Services

Innovative solutions for modern construction challenges

Specialized Construction Services

We serve commercial buildings, residential complexes, industrial facilities, and infrastructure projects.

Automated Plastering, Skimming & Rendering

Our specialized plastering systems deliver perfect finishes with remarkable consistency across all surfaces.

Why Choose Our Plastering Services?

Imagine walls and ceilings so smooth and flawless they look like they've been crafted by artists. Our robotic plastering technology applies the perfect amount of material every time, eliminating human error and creating surfaces that are ready for painting or wallpapering without any additional preparation.

This isn't just about aesthetics - it's about saving time and money while achieving results that manual methods simply can't match.

Steel Fixing & Shuttering

Specialized steel fixing solutions for precise reinforcement placement in all structural elements.

The Backbone of Your Structure

Think of steel reinforcement as the skeleton of your building - it provides the strength and stability that keeps everything standing strong for decades. Our automated systems place and secure steel reinforcements with millimeter precision, ensuring your structure can withstand the test of time and environmental stresses.

With our technology, we create frameworks that are perfectly aligned and securely fastened, giving you peace of mind about the integrity of your construction.

Concrete Structures & Foundations

Advanced concrete placement and finishing with specialized tools for durable, high-quality results.

The Foundation of Excellence

Concrete is the lifeblood of modern construction, and how it's placed and finished makes all the difference. Our advanced equipment ensures concrete is perfectly mixed, placed, and finished to create surfaces that are not only strong but also beautifully level and smooth.

From foundations that will support generations to come to floors that feel solid underfoot, our concrete work sets the standard for quality and durability in every project we undertake.

Subcontracting with Quality Assurance

APS provides reliable subcontracting services in formwork, finishes, and construction consultancy.

Your Trusted Construction Partner

When you partner with APS for subcontracting, you're not just hiring a service - you're gaining a dedicated team committed to excellence in every aspect of your project. We bring our expertise in formwork, finishes, and construction management to ensure every detail meets the highest standards.

Our quality assurance process is rigorous and thorough. We conduct comprehensive concrete strength testing at critical stages (7 and 28 days) and employ advanced moisture assessment techniques to identify potential issues before they become problems. This proactive approach ensures your investment is protected for the long term.
