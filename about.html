About APS

Innovative automated solutions for modern construction

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Advanced Plastering Services (APS) has been at the forefront of construction automation in Zimbabwe. We recognized the need for precision, efficiency, and safety in construction processes, leading us to develop specialized solutions.

Our team of engineers and construction experts work together to deliver services that reduce construction time, improve quality, and enhance safety on job sites.

Our Mission

To revolutionize Zimbabwe's construction industry through innovative technologies that deliver superior quality, faster project completion, and cost-effective solutions.
